Noodles

Description

Noodles are a versatile and quick dish that can be prepared in many ways. For a simple noodle recipe, start by boiling the noodles in salted water until tender. While the noodles cook, prepare a flavorful sauce using soy sauce, garlic, and a bit of oil. Stir-fry vegetables such as bell peppers, carrots, and onions in a hot pan with some oil.

Once the noodles are cooked, drain them and add them to the stir-fried vegetables. Pour in the sauce and toss everything together until the noodles are well coated. Serve hot, and enjoy your tasty noodle dish!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Garlic cloves(minced)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Carrots
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and add the noodles. Cook according to the package instructions (usually 5-7 minutes) until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and sesame oil. Set the sauce aside.>
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Then, add the sliced onions, bell peppers, and carrots. Stir-fry the vegetables for 3-4 minutes until they are slightly tender but still crisp.
  4. Once the vegetables are cooked, add the drained noodles to the pan. Pour the prepared sauce over the noodles and toss everything together.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir-fry the noodles for another 2-3 minutes until the noodles are well coated with the sauce and heated through.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions or sesame seeds if desired. Serve hot and enjoy your flavorful noodles!
