Burger

Description

To make a delicious burger, start by preparing the patty. Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and your preferred seasonings, then shape the mixture into round patties. Heat a grill or pan over medium-high heat and cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired doneness. While the patties are cooking, toast the burger buns in the oven or on the grill.

Once the patties are done, place them on the bottom bun, then add your toppings like cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles. Finish with your favorite sauces like ketchup, mayo, or mustard, and place the top bun. Serve your homemade burger with a side of fries or salad, and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Beef or Chicken
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Burger Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes(Sliced)
  • Onions(Sliced)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and any other seasonings of your choice. Form the mixture into round patties.
  2. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side or until they reach your preferred doneness.
  3. While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns in the oven, toaster, or on the grill.
  4. Place the cooked patty on the bottom bun. Add toppings like lettuce, sliced tomatoes, onions, pickles, and cheese if desired.
  5. Spread ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, or other sauces on the top bun
  6. Place the top bun over the patty and toppings. Serve with fries or a side of your choice.
